旧时只言冬晴好，
今日却盼春满城。
待到杨絮纷吹去，
风雨晴中风雨情。

In days of yore, we praised the clear winter sky,
Now we long for spring to fill the city wide.
Awaiting when the willow fluffs have blown away,
Through storms and calm, our bond remains, come what may.
